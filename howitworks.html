Why It Matters

Diverse teams drive performance. Companies with gender-balanced management teams perform better financially and demonstrate superior team dynamics and productivity. An analysis of 2,360 global companies across industries found that companies with women on their executive boards outperformed those with all-male executive boards.(1)

Startups benefit from diverse management. In an experiment where 12-person teams of entrepreneurs had to start up, sell stock for, and actually run 43 real companies with the goal of maximizing profit and shareholders’ value, statistical analysis found gender-balanced teams performed best. (2)

Collective Intelligence is the general ability of a group to perform a wide variety of tasks. Researchers have studied hundreds of people working in teams of 2-5 people and found collective intelligence to steadily increase with the number of women in the group.
